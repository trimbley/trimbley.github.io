About the author

Zhou Yu (周屿) maintains this open-source browser fingerprint & User-Agent viewer. The explanatory content is compiled from public research (EFF Panopticlick, AmIUnique, the "Hiding in the Crowd" study) and MDN Web Docs; the live scan reads only your own browser and uploads nothing.

Contact / corrections: open an issue on the project repository.
